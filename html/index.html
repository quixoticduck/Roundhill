Small companies may not have the benefit of an in-house accounts department, and bookkeeping can be time consuming and sometimes difficult and complex.

Roundhill Bookkeeping provides a specialist bookkeeping service to small businesses, listening to and appreciating every aspect of a client's needs, recognising that all are different and must be catered for individually.

About

About Us

Clive Baldwin AICB CB Cert PM Dip., the proprietor of Roundhill Bookkeeping, is a qualified bookkeeper regulated by The Institute of Certified Bookkeepers.

He has over 10 years experience as a freelance bookkeeper, working mostly with Sage Line 50.

Roundhill Bookkeeping is certified and authorised by The Institute of Certified Bookkeepers, and is fully insured.


How We Work

I supply computerised bookkeeping services for small businesses. This bookkeeping can be provided at a schedule which suits you depending on your business and its financial needs, working either from your office, or I can collect and work from mine.

We work alongside your accountancy professional - bookkeeping and accountancy are complimentary fields and having an efficient, effective relationship with your chosen accountancy firm will ultimately save you time and money.


Services

  • Maintaining accurate nominal sales and purchase ledgers
  • Reconciling your bank accounts
  • Preparing VAT returns
  • Preparation for and attendance at a Vat inspection
  • Management Accounts
  • Providing all bookkeeping services prior to year end accounts
  • Payroll
  • Paying creditors
  • Cash Flow

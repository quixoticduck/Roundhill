Spark of Resistance

A 60 Minutes Escape Room Game

The Game

Spark of Resistance is an exciting, interactive live escape game in Portland, Oregon for eight people.

An enigmatic secret agent locks you in a room. Everything in the room is a mystery to be solved or a clue to discover.

You and your friends, colleagues or family must use your wits and skills to work together and make your escape!

Who is it for?

Team building
Families and friends (ages 14+)
People who like games
Classrooms

Race the clock.

You have one hour from the time you set foot in the room to get through the puzzles, execute your mission and make your escape. Pulse-pounding fun!

Electrify your brain.

Spark of Resistance is designed to be challenging but fun. Great for all ages and levels of puzzle-solving experience, from novice to expert.

Interactive adventure.

This escape the room game combines story, theater and puzzle-solving for an amazing, interactive experience.

The Story

You and your team are secret agents who have been activated to uncover information about a colleague who has disappeared. You have one hour to investigate the strange, secretive government office that holds the answers. Can you solve the mystery and make it out in time?

Team Size

Eight people are allowed in the room at one time. If you would like to organize a session of the room with a larger party, such as for a birthday or corporate team-building, please contact us: [email protected]

What if my team is small? What if I want to come alone?

You may purchase any number of tickets here. The other slots in that session will be available for others to purchase, and we try to fill the room completely for each session.

Age Limits

Spark of Resistance is recommended for ages 13+.

Is it hard to solve clues? What if I get stuck on a puzzle?

Spark of Resistance is designed to be challenging in a fun way. Staff members will be on hand to give hints.

What do I need to bring?

You don’t need to bring anything! Paper, pens and pencils will be provided. There will be a secure space to store bags and coats.

Timing and Arrivals

Please arrive 15 minutes before the start of your session.
